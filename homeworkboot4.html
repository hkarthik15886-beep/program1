What is Nike's return policy?
Nike provides a 60-day trial period for most purchases made online (Nike App/Nike.com) or in Nike stores, allowing returns or exchanges within this time frame.
Do Nike shoes have a warranty?
Yes, Nike stands behind its products. If your Nike shoes develop a material or workmanship flaw, you can often file a claim.
How do I return items purchased from another retailer?
You must return items purchased from another retailer (e.g., an authorized Nike seller) to that specific retailer, following their return policy. If the retailer directs you back to Nike, you can use the claim submission form on the Nike website.
