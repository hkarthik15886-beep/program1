The Benefits of Owning Your Own Business

By Susan Ward

There are plenty of challenges that come with operating your own business. For many people, though, the rewards of running a business far outweigh the difficulties. In fact, 76% of those who decide to run their own small business are "somewhat happy" or "very happy" with their decision, according to a 2020 survey conducted by Guidant Financial's Small Rucinace Transic Alliance.
